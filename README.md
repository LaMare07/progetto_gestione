# 🎬 SeriesHub

A modern web platform where TV series fans can track what they’ve watched, rate their favorite shows, and discuss opinions with other users in real time.

![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/status-active-success.svg)

---

## 📖 About The Project

SeriesHub is a social platform designed for TV series lovers.  
Users can:

- ✅ Mark TV series as watched
- ⭐ Give ratings and reviews
- 💬 Chat and discuss opinions with other users
- 📚 Build their personal watchlist
- 🔍 Discover trending and highly rated series

The goal of the project is to create a community-driven experience where users can share recommendations, opinions, and reactions about their favorite shows.

---

## ✨ Features

### 👤 User Features
- User registration & authentication
- Personal profile page
- Watch history tracking
- Favorite series list
- Rating system (1–10 or stars)
- Reviews and comments

### 💬 Community Features
- Public discussion chats
- Real-time messaging
- Reply and reaction system
- Community recommendations

### 📺 TV Series Features
- Search TV shows
- Trending & popular series
- Genre filtering
- Series details and descriptions
- Episode and season tracking
